About IBA Music Olympiad 4.0

IBA Music Olympiad was a homegrown idea that turned huge in the year of its inception. Amongst all the academic and entrepreneurial events that go on at IBA, IBA Music Olympiad was aimed to bring vibrancy to the otherwise dull routine of musicians, dancers and even those who simply just enjoy rhythm and the beat. By mainly targeting the youth of Pakistan to create something creative and unique, IMO aims to provide a sense of energetic positivity in our society. The Olympiad is based on several competitions based on the various categories of music-based contests. Consisting of 4 different competitions, each with its separate prize money, the collective sum equals Rs, 100,000/-. Each contest will have several stages with the participants reaching the final round getting the prize money. The categories are defined in a manner that they will showcase every genre of music going into the very depth of the beauty of music. IBA Music Olympiad 4.0 will stretch over 3 days starting on the 29th of March 2019 and ending on 31st of March 2019.

About IMS

The IBA Music Society (IMS) since its commencement has been working towards the promotion of various forms of music, instrumental and vocal performances. This is very important, particularly for the student body since it gives them an opportunity to showcase their musical talents and bring out the inner musician in them.

A combination of fun and entertainment, the IMS holds some of the most exciting event at IBA which offer a perfect break to students from their hectic schedules. In the past, several events have been organized by the IMS which have been quite successful. Some of these include:

  • 1) IBA Voice Hunt:

    An intra-IBA competition to build up and encourage the vocal abilities and talents of IBA’s student body by offering them an opportunity to showcase their expertise. The new edition of this event is called IBA IDOL.

  • 2) IBA Base Rock Café:

    A series of sessions held to enhance instrumental performance and teach IBA students how to use a variety of different musical instruments ranging from guitars to drums.

  • 3) IBA Music Olympiad

    IMS has been organizing this mega event since the past three years, in which students from schools and universities compete against each other in various competitions like singing, dancing, beat boxing and battle of the bands.

The IMS also represents IBA at various national level Music conferences and competitions such as the LUMS Music Festival held in Lahore at the Lahore University of Management Sciences and at other universities Like NED University and Iqra University.

Furthermore, the IMS organized concerts at events organized by other student societies of IBA, from Noori at Charity Carnival 2013 to Strings at MUNIK VI.
